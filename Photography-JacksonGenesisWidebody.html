Jackson's Widebody Genesis

Info & Background

Subject: Jackson's 2014 Hyundai Genesis 2.0T *widebody*

Camera: Sony a6400
Lens(es): KamLan 50mm F/1.1 II
Date: September 11 and 18, 2020
Place: College Station, Texas

Background: This was another photoshoot I floated the idea of after he self-installed his widebody kit. Because of COVID-19, it was a little more difficult because of the obvious. We did practice proper social distancing. I did take the photos at like 4 AM for the night portion of the shoot just so traffic would clear up. College Station is a college town so people are up at all weird hours of the night. 4 AM was the average time that the streets started to clear out and allow for us to take pics.
