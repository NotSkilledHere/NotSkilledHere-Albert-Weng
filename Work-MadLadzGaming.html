MadLadzGaming

Info & Background

Client: MadLadzGaming
Product(s): Discord server, Twitter banner, Twitch banner, Twitch description banners

Background: In late 2020, my friend Devin, MadLadzGaming, started streaming and had voiced his desire to get some banners made to go with his stream. He had already started going around asking people to draw things or render things for him. However, when he showed it to me, while the cartoon character looked alright, the background just simply looked less than even lackluster to me. I offered to essentially do more for him for much less, obviously on the basis that because we are friends I'll give him a discount. I made it clear to him that I was not good at drawing these cartoon characters so he would have to source a satisfactory cartoon character somewhere else. This started as doing his Twitch and Twitter banners.

From there, he talked about wanting to get some Twitch description banners. Having seen the shitshow he went through for the Twitch and Twitter banners, I again offered to do those banners for him. In addition, he had wanted to make a discord server. Because of this, I offered to make the server for him as I have set up quite a few servers of my own before. As such, this resulted in all the current work I have done for him as of December 20, 2020.
