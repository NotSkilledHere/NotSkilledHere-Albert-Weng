Info & Background

Client: GhostToLane
Product(s): Only the video. Thumbnail not made by me.

Background: In early 2020, Gio (GhostToLane) put out a general @everyone in his Discord server asking for editors or people that knew any editors. I reached out and bascially said hey, I'd like to try my hand at editing something. He sent me a game to edit, which is featured here. Unfortunately, it wasn't exactly the most intense or highlight-reel generating game, but I gave a hand at it. His response was that it was really well editted. However, the general price range was above what he would want to pay per video. As such, he published this video, paid me, and we parted ways. Since its release, he has unlisted the video, maybe to keep the more popular video up and from getting buried.
